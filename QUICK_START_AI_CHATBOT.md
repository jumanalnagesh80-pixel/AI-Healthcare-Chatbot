## 💡 PRO TIPS

1. **Be Specific:** "I have 101°F fever for 2 days" gets better response than "I'm sick"

2. **Multiple Symptoms:** AI analyzes combinations intelligently

3. **Express Emotion:** "I'm worried about..." gets empathetic response

4. **Ask Follow-ups:** AI remembers context from previous messages

5. **Use Natural Language:** No need for medical terms - "my head hurts" works perfectly
